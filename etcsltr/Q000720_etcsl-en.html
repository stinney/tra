A šir-gida to Ninurta (Ninurta A)

Segment A

1-8. The warrior, the lordly son of Enlil, Ninurta, the fierce bull, fit to be a prince, the hero manifest in E-šu-me-ša, the glory of E-kur, the rigorous judge, king, …… of the gods, the butting bull, placing his foot on the rebel lands, Ninurta, the lord of E-šu-me-ša, has taken his seat on the throne-dais of An.

9-17. Like the new moon he comes forth over the people. Like Nanna he is …… in heaven and earth.
